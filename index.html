Tech Career Days

Tech Career Days is an annual two-day tech job fair organized by IT society of Multimedia University. Through this event, we aim to build a solid connection between the IT industry and MMU students, thereby achieving the following goals:

Help MMU students secure internships and full-time employments upon graduation, as well as freelance opportunities

Increase the exposure of MMU students regarding the latest IT job trends and industry expectations

Provide a platform for companies to find the technical talents they need to enhance their company's brand awareness among the youths

IT Society has been organizing this event since the year 2013 and has since helped many students securing job opportunities throughout the university. Unlike previous events, this annual event would be considered the largest scale of virtual events hosted by IT Society. This year, we will conduct this event virtually through Google Meet and Discord. Register now!

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I join the event?

Our event is held on a discord server, you will need to register for a participant account to view the server invitation HERE and be able to receive more information about the event (via Discord).

If I’m looking for a job, how should I submit my resume?

You may submit your resume on our resume submission portal. Before that, you will need to register an account first. Next, login into the resume submission portal, and submit your resume there.

I’m not an MMU student, can I join?

Yes, this event is open to everyone!

What are the activities that will be carried out in Tech Career Days?

We prepared some special events during the event day, including company presentation, resume workshop, informative career talk, tech moment (conference), pop quiz and prize giving. To view the more details about our event, register now to find out more!.

Is this event free for all?

Not to fret, admission is free.
